Egyptian Museum

The Egyptian Museum in Cairo is one of the most famous museums in the world. It has thousands of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including the treasures of King Tutankhamun. Tourists love it because they can see real mummies and ancient history.

Book Now

Grand Egyptian Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) is a new and modern museum near the pyramids. It will be the biggest archaeological museum in the world. Visitors enjoy the mix of ancient history and modern design.

Book Now

National Museum of Egyptian Civilization

This museum shows the full story of Egyptian civilization from ancient to modern times. The Royal Mummies Hall is very popular with tourists.

Book Now

Coptic Museum

The Coptic Museum focuses on Christian history in Egypt. It has beautiful old art, manuscripts, and religious objects. Foreign visitors like it because it is peaceful and unique

Book Now

Museum of Islamic Art

This museum has one of the biggest collections of Islamic art in the world. You can see old books, pottery, and decorations. Tourists love the detailed designs.

Book Now

Luxor Museum

Luxor Museum is small but very organized. It has high-quality statues and artifacts from ancient Thebes. Visitors like it because it is easy to explore

Book Now

Nubian Museum

This museum shows the culture and history of Nubia. It explains how Nubian people lived. Tourists enjoy learning about a different side of Egypt.

Book Now

Alexandria National Museum

This museum tells the story of Alexandria from ancient to modern times. It is inside a beautiful old palace. Visitors like its mix of history and architecture.

Book Now

Royal Jewelry Museum

This museum shows the jewelry of Egypt’s royal family. It is inside a luxurious palace. Tourists love the gold and diamonds and the elegant building.
